About dateCent

dateCent is the nCent Labs incentive protocol that incentivizes your friends to find the perfect match for you. Your friends know you better than anyone, why not let them set you up?




Compete with Friends

Who can find you a date first? Make finding your match a game to your friends. Let them compete to find you the best date possible.

Find your match

Your friends can find you a better date than any dating website or service. Let them set you up and they'll recieve rewards if you find your match.

Expand Your Network

Take advantage of your friends' networks to meet new people. Expand your circle of friends and become more integrated in your community.

More options

With all your friends working to find you a date, you'll have more options than ever before.
