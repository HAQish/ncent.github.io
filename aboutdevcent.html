devCent
devCent is an experimental recruiting tool under development by nCent Labs.
Here’s how devCent works:
  • Let’s pretend Alice receives a devCent
  • If Alice applies to nCent and is hired, she can redeem her devCent for a $10k bonus
  • Alice can instead send the devCent to Bob
  • If Bob is hired, he can redeem his devCent for a $10k signing bonus and Alice will receive a $5k bonus
  • Bob can instead send the devCent to Carol
  • If Carol is hired, she can redeem her devCent for a $10k signing bonus in which case Bob will receive a $5k bonus and Alice will receive a $2.5k bonus
  • And so on!
nCent Labs is a Stanford / Google / MIT PhD-led blockchain startup backed by Sequoia, SV Angel, Winklevoss Capital, MetaStable, Naval Ravikant, Steve Jurvetson, and others. We have a bold mission to create a freer and fairer decentralized internet where the users directly benefit from the network effects they create and own. To do this, we are launching a novel new blockchain that has viral incentives built into the protocol. We have a fast-paced culture and are focused on shipping robust code.
A few important rules:
  • You can only have one devCent at a time
  • In order to be eligible for a bonus, you need to be eligible to work in the United States
  • Don’t send your devCent if you’re still in the application process! You need to still have your devCent when you join the company.
  • You must have been working at nCent for at least six months before you can redeem your nCent
