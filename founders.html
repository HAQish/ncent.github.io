nCent Global Campus

Connecting students around the world to nCent Labs.

About

Our mission is to promote knowledge exchange about Blockchain, nCent, and related technologies by connecting with college students. We also aim to create a network between colleges of students interested in Blockchain technologies and related fields. With nCent Global Campus, we hope to extend the pipeline of the Blockchain community through inclusionary and diversification efforts.

Founder's Program

Become more involved with nCent Labs at your university.

About the Program

The nCent Global Campus Founder's Program is an organization of university-based blockchain clubs and initiatives to support nCent Nation. Email our founder (kk@ncnt.io) to find our more.

Benefits

* Connect your campus with nCent Labs. nCent will organize speaking events with our top-notch team of developers.
* Get a first look at our new releases.
Receive invitations to our conference and exclusive networking opporunities.
* Earn a leadership role in organizing the buildCent grants for your university.

Values

* Education: your club will promote understanding and adopting Blockchain technology.
* Practice: Organize hackathons, challenges, white paper reading groups and other events to exercise your knowledge about Blockchain.
* Diversity and Inclusivity: We want to help make the Blockchain community one that is diverse and inclusive.

nCent Labs

nCent Labs is a Stanford University spinout blockchain research and development company. We are focused on building novel, protocol-level technologies that help people connect more efficiently. Our core belief is that you have the right to own the value of the networks you create. We invite you to connect with us and support our mission to build a fairer and freer decentralized internet!
