Participation Process

1. Please read our litepaper

2. Fill out our application form for the dates you are available. You can sign up for as many dates as you like.

    a.  October 6
    b.  November 3
    c.  December 1
    d.  January 5
    e.  February 2
    f.   March 2

3. Two weeks prior to each event, we will send all applicants for that date a form with detailed information about the projects for that month’s HackCent. Please indicate your project preferences on that form.

4. Your application and project preferences will be reviewed by the team leaders. We will message you shortly with a team assignment and any “homework” that might be required.

5. Prior to hackCent Saturday morning, please complete the “homework” so we can hit the ground running.

6. Saturday: HACK!!!

nCent Labs

 nCent Labs is a Stanford University spinout blockchain research and development company. We are focused on building novel, protocol-level technologies that help people connect more efficiently. Our core belief is that you have the right to own the value of the networks you create. We invite you to connect with us and support our mission to build a fairer and freer decentralized internet!
