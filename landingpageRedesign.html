What is nCent?

nCent will establish a new cryptocurrency, NCNT, which will exist on a decentralized base layer protocol. NCNT lets users easily create, share and participate in incentive applications represented as smart contracts. These tokenized smart contracts (or “stamped tokens”) unlock the collective value of our networks. Incentive applications thrive given the trust, value attribution, and Sybil resistance afforded by blockchain technology.

Incentivizing Virality without Sacrificing Security

Networks go viral when the right incentives are in place. We aim to reward people for (i) joining and recruiting new users to your network (ii) maintaining the validity and security of your network (iii) providing specialized services that benefit your network. Since nCent is specifically optimized for scalability, auditability, and resistance to fraud, the virality it affords doesn’t sacrifice network security.

Applications Abound

nCent itself was successfully grown using incentive programs. Featured applications include customer acquisition, employment search, fundraising, crowdwork campaigns, social networks, online auctions, and more.

Market Opportunities

Acquire More Customers

Seed some discount coins for items you want to sell. Participants receive a discount when they purchase the item while holding a coin and receive a reward whenever someone they transfer a coin to purchases the item.

Recruit the Ideal Employee

Seed some recruitment coins for open positions in your business. Participants receive a reward when they are hired while holding a coin and whenever someone they transfer a coin to is hired.

Maximize Charitable Donations

Seed some pledge coins to invite donations in exchange for an appreciation package. Participants can pledge to donate by transferring the coin or donate immediately. When an immediate donation occurs, everyone who has pledged by transferring immediately donates and receives an appreciation package.

Create a Social Network

Seed some coins to people using a dating app. Coins grant in-app benefits. Participants get more coins by using their network to match-make, growing the application's network.
